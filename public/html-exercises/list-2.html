Blueberry Cheescake

Ingredients

For crust
  • 2 1/3 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/4 cup sugar
    • For filling
  • 4 8-ounce packages cream cheese
  • 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 5 large eggs
  • 1 16-ounce container sour cream
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
    • For topping
  • 2 6-ounce baskets fresh blueberries
  • 1/3 cup all-fruit blueberry spread

    • Preparation

    Make crust
    1. Position rack in center of oven and preheat to 375 F.
    2. Wrap outside 10-inch-diameter springform pan with heavy-duty foil.
    3. Combine all ingredients in processor and blend using on/off turns until crumbs are moist.
    4. Press crumb mixture firmly onto bottom and 2 inches up sides of prepared pan.
    5. Bake until crust begins to brown, about 8 minutes, and transfer crust to rack and cool.
    Make filling
    1. Using electric mixer, beat cream cheese and sugar in a large bowl until well blended.
    2. Beat in flour and add eggs 1 at a time, beating just until combined.
    3. Beat in sour cream, milk, and vanilla.
    4. Pour filling into crust.
    5. Place springform pan in a large roasting pan.
    6. Pour enough water to the roasting pan to come 1 inch up sides of the pan.
    7. Bake cheescake until just set in center and center is slight puffed and golden brown.
    8. Turn off oven; keep door closed; let cheescake stand in oven for 1 hour.
    9. Remove cheescake from roasting pan and refrigerate until cold.
    10. Cover; refrigerate overnight.
    Make topping
    1. Stir blueberry spread in large skillet over low heat until melted.
    2. Remove from hear and add blueberries.
    3. Spoon blueberry mixture over top of cake, spreading evenly.
    4. Chill until cold and transfer cake to platter.
