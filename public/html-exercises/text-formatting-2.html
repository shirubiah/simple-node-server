The Tintin Ronolo

My name is Sylvia Christine M. Ronolo, but nobody really calls me Sylvia or Christine. They call me Tintin instead. I was born in February 16, 1993 at Batangas City, Philippines. I am a full-blooded Filipino but my heart and mind belongs to the different countries of the world. I love learning foreign languages, culture, and traditions and I dream of being able to travel around the world.


I spent my highschool years in CSB and pursued BA Communication Arts latter on at UPLB. I like quite a lot of books. Some of my favorite books would be the Harry Potter Series by JK Rowling, An Abundance of Katherines by John Green, Boy Meets Boy by David Levithan, Charlie and the Chocolate Factory by Roald Dahl, 13 Reasons Why by Jay Asher, and The Bell Jar by Sylvia Plath.

I remember the time we were given a project of lettering any quotation we like, and I immediately looked for quotations from Mark Twain (don't quite remember why I specifically wanted Twain). I chose his quotation Be good and you will be lonesome. which I found profound and very intgriguing. It was short, simple, yet it has quite an impact. I still think of that quotation from time to time.
